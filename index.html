Bloodbath

A first person hack-and-slash action game about killing things and absorbing their souls to increase your stats. 
You play as Vandred, a lab-grown superhuman who was basically supposed to become the standard soldier for humanity, 
but when they tried bonding you to a hell artifact called the Bloodblade, shit hit the fan. 
Combat is designed to be fast and frantic; parrying gives back health, and the Bloodblade grants buffs for killing enemies. 
Think ULTRAKILL merged with Devil May Cry. 
Oh, and your clone/rival/brother is actively hunting you down.
